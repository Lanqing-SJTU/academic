    Lanqing Yang (杨岚青)

    Associate Researcher at Mobile Intelligent Networks and Systems (Minds) Lab, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

    Research Interests: Internet of Things, Intelligent Sensing, Large Language Models.

    Introduction: Supervised by Professor Guangtao Xue, a Changjiang Scholar of the Ministry of Education. Published 20+ papers in CCF A/B conference such as USENIX SECURITY, NSDI, and MOBICOM. Received the 2021 Excellent Paper Nomination Award at World Artificial Intelligence Conference.

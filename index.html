Bio

I join Shanghai Jiao Tong University as a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering in 2018. I received my B.S. and M.S. in the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Taiwan University in 2004 and 2006, respectively. I got my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin in 2015. Prior to joining SJTU, I spent a year as a Researcher in Huawei Future Network Theory Lab in Hong Kong and then worked as a Co-founder in Hauoli LLC. My research interests focus on networked systems and span the areas of wireless networking, network measurement and analytics, and mobile computing.
